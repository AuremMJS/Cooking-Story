Game Description:

This is a game to simulate two chefs in a restaurant.

Two Players has to complete the salad orders placed by customers in the queue within the specified time.

Not providing the customers with the correct salad on time will add negative points to the players.

Players cannot move when a vegetable is being chopped.

Player can place a vegetable in the plate next to chopping board when needed to carry the salad.
